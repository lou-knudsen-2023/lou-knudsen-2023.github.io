My Learning Plan





What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My short term plan is to get a job specifically where I can double down on my learning and really cement what I have learnt. Long term I feel like there could be something that brings in my skills as UX researcher into the software development world. I don't know what that is yet, but I have faith that there is something out there for me. For the most part, I'm trying to keep my mind on this course. (I have a tendency to over think things!)


A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I am organised, super organised. I'm good at juggling multuple things at once. I think visually. When someone explains something to me in code, I have to visualise it and then it makes sense. This slows me down a bit. I also think a limitation of mine is that I like to understand the big picture, and quite often will rabbit hole.


What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I really want to learn how to think like a developer and know how to empathise with that world. What are the stressors that software developers encounter? Empathy is a mental muscle, so always looking to stretch that.
